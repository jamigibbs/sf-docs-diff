lightning-accordion-sectionmodule

A single section that is nested in an accordion component.

Support:GA
Access:global
Abstract:false
Extensible:false

Attributes

NameTypeAccessRequiredDefaultDescription
labelglobalThe text that displays as the title of the section.
nameglobalThe unique section name to use with the active-section-name attribute in the accordion component.
titleglobalReserved for internal use.

Slots

NameDescription
actionsPlaceholder for actionable components, such as lightning-button or lightning-button-menu. Actions are displayed at the top right corner of the accordion section.
defaultPlaceholder for your content in the accordion section.
