lightning-carouselmodule

A collection of images that are displayed one at a time.

Support:BETA
Access:global
Abstract:false
Extensible:false

Attributes

NameTypeAccessRequiredDefaultDescription
disable-auto-refreshglobalIf present, the carousel doesn't loop after the last image is displayed.
disable-auto-scrollglobalIf present, images do not automatically scroll and users must click the indicators to scroll.
scroll-durationglobal5The auto scroll duration. The default is 5 seconds, after that the next image is displayed.

Slots

NameDescription
defaultPlaceholder for carousel-image. Up to 5 carousel-image components are allowed.
